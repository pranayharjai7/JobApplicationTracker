# Privacy Policy for JobTrack

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

JobTrack ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Gmail API Data
JobTrack uses the Gmail API to scan your inbox for specifically job-related communications (applications, interview invites, offers, etc.). 
- **Scopes**: We use the `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly` scope.
- **Data Access**: The App reads email headers (Subject, From, Date) and body text to identify job application updates.

### Local Data
Most data processing occurs locally on your device. We store a local cache of your job applications and statuses in a secure on-device database.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Job Tracking**: To automatically populate your job application dashboard and journey visualization.
- **AI Summarization**: With your consent, email fragments are sent to AI providers (like Google Gemini) to extract specific details like company name, role, and salary. This data is not used for training models by our App.
- **Improved UX**: To provide smart filters and timeline views.

## 3. Data Integrity and Security

### On-Device Processing
Scanning and matching of emails happen primarily on your device. We do not maintain a central server that mirrors your inbox.

### Third-Party Disclosure
- **Google Services**: We use Google Play Services for authentication.
- **AI Providers**: If AI features are enabled, specific email text is sent to the AI provider for processing. Please refer to the AI provider's privacy policy for their data handling.
- **No Selling of Data**: We **never** sell, rent, or trade your Gmail data or personal information to third parties.

## 4. Limited Use Disclosure
JobTrack's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Service User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

## 5. Your Choices
- **Account Removal**: You can choose to remove your Google Account and delete all associated local data at any time via the "Account Settings" menu.
- **Data Wipe**: You can manually wipe all local application data at any time.

## 6. Hosting & Contact
For concerns regarding this policy, please contact us via the support channel in our Play Store listing.
